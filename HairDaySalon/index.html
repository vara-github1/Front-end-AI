Welcome to Hair Day,

Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that specializes in cut, color and styling. Hair Day also offers makeup and nail services, prides itself on its warm and relaxing atmosphere and is best known for edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts.

The Batch:

Two stylists (Pria,Garry), three colorists(Rum,Vodka,Gin), one manicurist(Whisky) and one make-up artist(Champagne).

Meet Your Hairdresser:

Pria, the owner of Hair Day, earned her certificate as a colorist and immediately started specializing in edgy, custom coloring techniques. She wanted to create a "treat yourself" environment for her clients.

Book Your Appointment!!!

Hair Day can only accommodate a small number of coloring appointments each week, so we suggest you to book your appointment in advance.
